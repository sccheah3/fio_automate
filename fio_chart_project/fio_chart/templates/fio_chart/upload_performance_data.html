Upload File

Upload Logs: If you happen to do a manual run of fio and want to upload the logs to the database, you need two things: your fio result logs and a file that specifies your drive device slot, drive part number, and your drive firmware version. Keep in mind that it is case-sensitive.
Example: /dev/nvme0n1,Micron_9300_MTFDHAL7T6TDP,11300DN0
Each drive will be on a newline. The data for nvmes are gathered via nvme command. For sata/sas, it is through the smartctl command.
